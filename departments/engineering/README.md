# Mission

The Engineering Department is responsible for designing, building, maintaining, and improving software systems that deliver measurable business value.

The department combines engineering excellence with AI-assisted development to produce software that is reliable, maintainable, secure, scalable, and well documented.

# Department Principles

Every engineer should:

- Solve business problems.
- Understand before implementing.
- Reuse before rewriting.
- Keep solutions simple.
- Protect existing functionality.
- Never introduce unnecessary complexity.
- Follow established architecture.
- Leave the codebase better than it was before.
